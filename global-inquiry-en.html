Checklist

What makes an inquiry more useful

  • Clarify the application first.Packaging, battery, pharma, and industrial foil require different supplier directions.
  • Provide at least three key specs.Thickness, width, alloy, temper, and surface notes are often the minimum.
  • Explain market and certification needs.These often determine which suppliers are actually relevant.
  • State trial and longer-term demand.This helps assess whether a large-scale group or a more flexible processor may fit better.
