Buyer Desk

A clearer workflow for global aluminum foil buyers

This page explains how sourcing teams can use the platform to move from market understanding to supplier shortlist and inquiry action with better structure.

Buyer workflow

  • 1. Start with knowledge when technical clarity is needed.
  • 2. Browse suppliers by application focus and capability clues.
  • 3. Submit a structured inquiry for follow-up, sampling, and quotation.

What buyers usually need first

  • Application understanding across packaging, battery, pharma, HVAC, and industrial foil
  • Basic standards and quality indicators before supplier comparison
  • A shortlist logic that goes beyond price and product name alone

What to prepare before inquiry

  • Alloy, temper, thickness, width, and application requirements
  • Target market, certification, delivery port, and commercial terms
  • Sample, trial order, and annual volume expectations

Supplier discovery

Use the supplier directory to compare manufacturers by category, export profile, and capability direction.

Knowledge check

Use the knowledge base and AI Search to clarify process, standards, and application-specific questions.

Inquiry flow

Package the requirement clearly, then move into inquiry, sample discussion, and next-step coordination.
