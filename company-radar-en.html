Radar Themes

Three themes worth watching now

Battery foil remains the most watched growth path

Capacity alone is not enough. Qualification speed, production consistency, and customer structure still shape real procurement relevance.

Export capability is becoming more visible as a differentiator

Overseas buyers increasingly care about documentation, communication, compliance, and follow-through, not only price.

Application-specific strength still matters

HVAC, packaging, pharma, and battery foil often reward very different manufacturing strengths and quality systems.
